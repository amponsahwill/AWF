About Us

WE CREATED THE VILLAGE AS A SPACE FOR THE AFRICAN COMMUNITY TO COME TOGETHER AND SHARE CONNECTIONS AND IDEAS.

THIS IS A SPACE WHERE WE CAN HELP EACH OTHER ACADEMICALLY, SOCIALLY, AND ECONOMICALLY. THIS CAN BE ACHIEVED THROUGH NETWORKING!

IN THIS DAY-AND-AGE ITS NOT ABOUT WHAT YOU KNOW BUT WHO YOU KNOW. CONNECTING WITH THE RIGHT PEOPLE CAN OPEN DOORS TO OPPORTUNITIES THAT WERE ONCE CLOSED.

THE VILLAGE IS IMPORTANT BECAUSE THERE IS A LACK OF TOGETHERNESS WITHIN THE AFRICAN COMMUNITY.

GROWING UP WE ARE TAUGHT TO KEEP OUR PLANS TO OURSELVES OR KEEPING THE RESOURCES WE USED A SECRET.

THIS PRACTICE NEEDS TO COME TO AN END, ESPECIALLY HERE IN A FOREIGN LAND WHERE WE NEED TO BE ABLE TO LEAN ON EACH OTHER.
